Meet pocketSOHO


Real Time Messaging

Our in-app messenger allows you to communicate with your customers 24/7 because we know how important it is for your business. pochetSOHO consolidate all your communication, scheduling and payment in one simple app.

Instant (re)Scheduling

Scheduling, rescheduling and keeping track of appointments are often a headache for small business owners. See all your appointments at a glance, manage, schedule and re-schedule with just a few clicks.

Get paid on time

Because getting paid for your hard work should not be such a hassle, pochetSOHO designed a very simple in-app payment application allowing you to invoice, track payments, remind your customers and even helps you with reporting during tax time.

What's Inside

pocketSOHO helps SMALL or HOME Business owners run their business smoothly and efficiently. We bring together and simplify the repetitive task that sucks most of you time so you can enjoy spending more time doing what you love and making money!


  • Instant Message Customers
  • Schedule appointments
  • Send Invoices & Track Payments
  • Integrated & Secured Online Payment
  • Track & Visual Income
  • Speed-up Tax Reporting

Easiest way of combining the most needed 3 features to run a small office, home office, individual service business such as piano teacher, therapist, tutor, yoga teacher, nanny, and dog walker.
